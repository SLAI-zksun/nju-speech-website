2027 Fall 研究生招生说明（硕士 / 博士）2027 Fall Graduate Recruitment (Master & Ph.D.)

26-01-01 招生｜2027 Fall｜语音智能Recruitment · 2027 Fall · Speech AI

面向 2027 Fall 持续招收硕士与博士研究生；欢迎对说话人建模、目标说话人处理、语音/音频/音乐理解与生成、类脑语音处理、端到端语音大模型等方向感兴趣的同学邮件联系，并附上简历、成绩单与研究兴趣陈述。We recruit Master and Ph.D. students for 2027 Fall. If you are interested in speaker modeling, target speaker processing, speech/audio/music understanding and generation, brain-inspired speech processing, or end-to-end speech LLMs, please email with your CV, transcripts, and a brief statement of research interests.
