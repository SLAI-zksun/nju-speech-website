    <section id="sec-overview">
      <h2><span class="lang-zh">实验室概况</span><span class="lang-en">Lab Overview</span></h2>

      <p><span class="lang-zh">智能语音与音频处理实验室由王帅副教授牵头建设，依托南京大学智能科学与技术学院与深圳河套学院（SLAI）双平台，聚焦智能音频信号处理前沿研究，覆盖语音、音频、音乐等多模态声学信号方向，打造学术研究 — 技术研发 — 产业落地一体化创新平台。</span><span class="lang-en">The Intelligent Speech and Audio Processing Lab is led by Associate Professor Shuai Wang, anchored across <strong>NJU (School of Intelligence Science and Technology)</strong> and <strong>SLAI</strong>. We focus on frontier intelligent audio signal processing, covering multimodal acoustic signals including speech, audio, and music, and aim to build an integrated innovation platform connecting research, technology development, and real-world deployment.</span></p>

      <p><span class="lang-zh">实验室立足国际学术前沿，深耕说话人建模、目标说话人处理、语音合成与转换、音乐生成、端到端语音大模型等核心领域，主持国家自然科学基金青年项目、长三角科技创新共同体联合攻关子课题、人工智能教育部重点实验室开放课题等多项国家级、省部级科研项目。团队兼具深厚学术积累与产业实践经验，与腾讯、字节跳动、网易、Meta FAIR 等头部企业深度合作，推动科研成果高效转化，致力于培养具备全球视野、创新能力与工程素养的人工智能拔尖人才。</span><span class="lang-en">We work at the international research frontier on core topics such as speaker modeling, target speaker processing, speech synthesis and conversion, music generation, and end-to-end speech large models. The group has hosted multiple national and provincial-level projects (including the NSFC Young Scientists Fund, a Yangtze River Delta joint R&amp;D sub-project, and an MOE Key Laboratory open project). Combining strong academic foundations with industry experience, we collaborate closely with leading companies including Tencent, ByteDance, NetEase, and Meta FAIR to translate research outcomes, and we are committed to cultivating top AI talent with global perspective, creativity, and engineering excellence.</span></p>

      <p><span class="lang-zh">实验室仍在快速发展中，具体表述以学校、学院与合作机构的官方发布为准。</span><span class="lang-en">The group is still rapidly evolving; please treat official statements from Nanjing University, SLAI, and related schools/institutes as authoritative.</span></p>

    <section id="sec-research">
      <h2><span class="lang-zh">研究领域</span><span class="lang-en">Research Areas</span></h2>

      <p><span class="lang-zh">实验室聚焦智能音频信号处理全链路研究，覆盖语音、音频、音乐等多模态声学信号，形成了特色鲜明、布局合理的核心研究体系。核心研究方向围绕<a href="{{ '/research/' | relative_url }}">音频前端</a>、<a href="{{ '/research/acoustic-understanding/' | relative_url }}">音频理解</a>、<a href="{{ '/research/acoustic-generation/' | relative_url }}">音频生成</a>及<a href="{{ '/research/advanced-speech-intelligence/' | relative_url }}">前沿语音智能</a>四大板块展开，其中音频前端方向重点开展说话人建模、目标说话人提取、语音增强与分离、说话人识别与分群等相关研究，为各类下游语音处理任务提供高质量的信号支撑；音频理解方向面向语音、音频事件、音乐内容开展全方位智能分析，实现多场景下各类声学信息的精准解析与深度理解；音频生成方向专注于高保真语音合成、声音转换、音乐生成与音效创作等技术研发，着力打造自然流畅、高多样性的声学内容生成能力；前沿语音智能方向则聚焦类脑语音处理、端到端语音大模型、多模态语音交互等前沿交叉领域，持续推动下一代语音智能技术的突破性发展。</span><span class="lang-en">We pursue full-stack intelligent audio signal processing across multimodal acoustic signals (speech, audio, and music), organized into four pillars on this site: <a href="{{ '/research/' | relative_url }}">Audio Front-end</a>, <a href="{{ '/research/acoustic-understanding/' | relative_url }}">Audio Understanding</a>, <a href="{{ '/research/acoustic-generation/' | relative_url }}">Audio Generation</a>, and <a href="{{ '/research/advanced-speech-intelligence/' | relative_url }}">Advanced Speech Intelligence</a>. Audio front-end focuses on speaker modeling, target speaker extraction, enhancement and separation, speaker recognition and diarization, providing high-quality signal conditioning for downstream tasks. Audio understanding targets comprehensive analysis of speech, audio events, and music for accurate parsing and deep semantics across scenarios. Audio generation develops high-fidelity speech synthesis, voice conversion, music generation, and sound design toward natural and diverse acoustic content. Advanced speech intelligence explores brain-inspired speech processing, end-to-end speech LLMs, and multimodal spoken interaction to push next-generation speech intelligence.</span></p>

      <p><span class="lang-zh">更细的方向划分与代表性论文展示，可在网站的 <a href="{{ '/research/' | relative_url }}">研究方向</a> 与 <a href="{{ '/projects/' | relative_url }}">研究成果</a> 栏目中查看。</span><span class="lang-en">For a finer-grained direction map and representative outputs, please visit <a href="{{ '/research/' | relative_url }}">Research Directions</a> and <a href="{{ '/projects/' | relative_url }}">Research Outputs</a>.</span></p>
    </section>

    <section id="sec-highlights">
      <h2><span class="lang-zh">实验室特色</span><span class="lang-en">Highlights</span></h2>

      <p><span class="lang-zh">实验室秉持学术与产业并重的发展理念，深耕语音领域前沿理论研究，目前主持国家自然科学基金青年项目、长三角科技创新共同体联合攻关项目子课题、人工智能教育部重点实验室开放课题及CCF-网易雷火创新基金等多项国家级、省部级科研项目，在ICASSP、Interspeech等领域顶级会议和期刊发表论文60余篇，荣获ISCSLP 2024最佳论文奖、最佳学生论文奖，以及VoxSRC 2019、DIHARD 2019等国际竞赛多项冠军，学术影响力突出。同时，实验室依托南京大学与深圳河套学院双平台优势，联动腾讯、字节跳动、网易等头部企业资源，推动科研成果高效落地与转化，发起的WeSpeaker、WeSep等开源工具，其预训练模型在HuggingFace平台月均下载量超千万次，在学术界与工业界获得广泛应用。实验室采用开放协同的培养模式，与李海洲教授等国际知名学者联合指导学生，整合深港跨境科研资源，构建国际化、交叉学科、产学研协同的培养体系，鼓励学生深度参与前沿科研项目，优秀者可获得海内外顶尖高校深造及头部企业实习推荐，为学生提供多元发展平台与优质科研环境。</span><span class="lang-en">We pursue a balanced mission across academia and industry, advancing frontier speech research while hosting multiple national and provincial-level projects (including the NSFC Young Scientists Fund, a Yangtze River Delta joint R&amp;D sub-project, an MOE Key Laboratory open project, and the CCF–NetEase ThunderFire innovation fund). Our work appears regularly at top venues such as ICASSP and Interspeech (60+ papers), with recognitions including ISCSLP 2024 Best Paper and Best Student Paper awards, and championship-level results in international challenges such as VoxSRC 2019 and DIHARD 2019. Leveraging dual platforms at Nanjing University and SLAI, we collaborate closely with leading companies including Tencent, ByteDance, and NetEase to accelerate technology transfer. Open-source initiatives such as WeSpeaker and WeSep have reached broad adoption, with pretrained models downloaded on the order of tens of millions per month on Hugging Face. Our training model is open and collaborative: we co-advise students with internationally renowned scholars such as Prof. Haizhou Li, integrate cross-border research resources, and encourage deep participation in frontier projects—with strong pathways to further studies at top universities and internships at leading industry labs.</span></p>

      <ul class="compact-list">
        <li><span class="lang-zh"><strong>开源与影响力</strong>：团队围绕代表性问题构建开源工具与基准（如 WeSpeaker、WeSep、REAL-T 等），推动方法复现与社区协作。</span><span class="lang-en"><strong>Open science &amp; impact</strong>: We build open-source toolkits and benchmarks (e.g., WeSpeaker, WeSep, REAL-T) to improve reproducibility and community collaboration.</span></li>
        <li><span class="lang-zh"><strong>复杂真实场景</strong>：关注会议、对话、混叠与噪声等真实声学条件下的鲁棒性与可用性，强调从“理想数据集”走向“可部署系统”。</span><span class="lang-en"><strong>Real-world conditions</strong>: We care about robustness and usability in conversational, overlapped, and noisy environments—moving from ideal benchmarks toward deployable systems.</span></li>
        <li><span class="lang-zh"><strong>产学研协同</strong>：团队与国内外研究机构及产业界保持合作交流，重视学生系统能力培养与高质量实习实践机会。</span><span class="lang-en"><strong>Academia–industry synergy</strong>: We maintain collaborations across academia and industry, and emphasize strong engineering skills and high-quality internship experiences.</span></li>
      </ul>
    </section>
