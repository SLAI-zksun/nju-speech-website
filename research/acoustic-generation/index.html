音频生成Audio Generation

音频生成聚焦语音、音频与音乐的内容合成与可控创作，研究高保真语音合成、风格与情感可控生成、歌声与音乐生成等问题，强调音质、可控性、效率与工程可落地性。Audio generation focuses on controllable creation and synthesis for speech, audio, and music, including high-fidelity speech synthesis, style/emotion control, singing voice and music generation, with strong emphasis on quality, controllability, efficiency, and deployability.
