前沿语音智能Advanced Speech Intelligence

面向下一代语音智能，结合类脑语音处理与语音大模型两条路线，探索更具泛化能力、可交互能力与鲁棒性的统一建模方法，推动复杂真实场景下的语音理解与推理能力。This direction targets next-generation speech intelligence by combining brain-inspired processing and speech large models. We explore unified modeling with stronger generalization, interaction, and robustness for real-world speech understanding and reasoning.
