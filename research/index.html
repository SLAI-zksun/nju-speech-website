音频前端Audio Front-end

聚焦语音/音频任务中的关键前端处理能力，覆盖说话人建模与目标说话人处理两条主线，重点研究说话人表征学习、说话人识别与验证、目标说话人提取、语音分离以及复杂场景下的鲁棒增强。This direction targets core front-end capabilities for speech/audio systems, combining speaker modeling and target speaker processing. We focus on speaker representation learning, speaker verification, target speaker extraction, speech separation, and robust enhancement in challenging real-world conditions.
